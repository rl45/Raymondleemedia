About Me

As the son of two immigrant parents, I learned the importance of work-ethic from an early age. With their strong hard-working mentality engrained in me, I graduated from UC Santa Cruz with a Computer Science degree and started at a marketing internship at a nearby startup.

I quickly realized I wanted to make a larger impact with my career and also wanted to work directly with clients. So, I set out on my own.

As I started out, the foundation of my style and philosophies was rooted in marketing material that made one of biggest impacts on me as a person: Nike commercials.

These campaigns inspired me to train every day. Whether I was building on my foundational skills, or improving my fitness, I focused on growth.

Since launching my own business, I’ve been able to work with multiple companies to help them connect with their clients and customers through successful digital marketing campaigns. I look forward to helping your business as well.
