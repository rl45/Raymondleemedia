About Me

With both of my parents as immigrants, and small business owners, I saw the importance of work-ethic from an early age. However, despite their hard-work ethic, some days they wouldn’t even make a profit. This began my interest in business development and digital marketing.

With their strong work ethic developed in me, I got accepted to UC Santa Cruz for a Computer Science degree. During my sophomore year, I accepted a marketing internship at a nearby startup.

I quickly realized that I wanted a larger impact and to work directly with clients. At this point, Im wondering what kind of work should I do?

Through my self analysis, I found a reoccurring event that resonated with me the most: Nike commercials.

These campaigns inspired me to train everyday. Whether I was building on my foundational skills, or improving my fitness, I focused on growth.

So with this motivation to re-create the feeling I had growing up, I cold called and emailed hundreds of local businesses for three months. Restaurants. Agencies. Artists. Any business I could help.

And that’s where the story began…
